Ques2:What are the uses of JavaScript Operators?

Ans:Operators are used to assign values, compare values, perform arithmetic operations, and more.

Ques3:Explain the Assignment Operator.

Ans:The assignment operator = assigns the value of its right-hand operand to a variable, a property, or an indexer element given by its left-hand operand.

Ques4:Explain the all Arithmetic Operators.

Ans:The arithmetic operators perform addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, exponentiation, and modulus operations.

Ques5:What are the Binary Operators in js.

Ans:Binary operators in JS require two operands for operation. If one or more than two operands are provided, such operators result in a syntax error.

Ques6:Explain the Pre-Increment and Post Increment in js.

Ans:Pre-increment means first increment then use and post-increment means first use and then increment
